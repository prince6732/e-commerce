## Technical Stack Recommendations

### For Laravel Backend:
- **Queue System**: Laravel Horizon for job processing
- **Caching**: Redis for session and cache management
- **Search**: Algolia or Meilisearch for fast product search
- **Email**: Amazon SES or SendGrid
- **Storage**: Amazon S3 for images
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics 4, Mixpanel

### For Next.js Frontend:
- **State Management**: Zustand or Redux Toolkit
- **Forms**: React Hook Form (already using)
- **Charts**: Recharts or Chart.js
- **Image Optimization**: Next.js Image component
- **SEO**: next-seo package
- **Analytics**: Vercel Analytics

## Success Metrics to Track

1. **Conversion Rate**: % of visitors who make a purchase
2. **Average Order Value (AOV)**: Average amount per order
3. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total revenue per customer
4. **Cart Abandonment Rate**: % of carts not completed
5. **Return Customer Rate**: % of customers who return
6. **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost to acquire one customer
7. **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Customer satisfaction metric
8. **Page Load Time**: Website performance
9. **Mobile vs Desktop Sales**: Device breakdown
10. **Top Traffic Sources**: Where customers come from

## Conclusion

This document provides a comprehensive roadmap for improving your e-commerce platform. Start with high-priority features that directly impact revenue and user experience, then gradually implement medium and low-priority features based on your resources and business goals.

Remember:

- **Get user feedback** regularly
- **Monitor analytics** to measure success
- **Iterate and improve** continuously
- **Keep security as top priority**
